SALON POLICIES

Cancellation Policy

Appointments are highly recommended as well as pre-booking your next appointment upon checkout. We offer a reminder call the day before your appointment. As a courtesy to our other guests, please notify the staff 24 hours in advance if you need to reschedule an appointment. We keep our prices affordable, and therefore 50% service charge will be applied for same day cancellations or no shows payable upon your next visit. Prices & policies are subjest to change without notice.

Child Policy

We love children, but due to safety hazards present in our environment such as sharp implements, hot wax and chemicals, we are unable to accommodate unattended children under the age of 12 in our salon unless they have a scheduled appointment. Nor do our facilities have the capacity to accommodate carriers and strollers. Anyone under the age of 18 receiving a service for the first time must have a parent or guardian present at the initial appointment to give written consent. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause, but appreciate your understanding in this matter!

Eek! I'm afraid!

We understand there is anxiety and nervousness for first time visitors, but due to board standards set by the state of Virginia, only one person may be in a service room at a time.
