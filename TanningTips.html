TANNING TIPS

To Prepare

Exfoliate your entire body either with a washcloth or a non-oil based body scrub. Pay special attention to the elbows, knees & ankles as these areas tend to be drier. Do this to your face as well. Remember to shave during this time as you don’t want to do this after your airbrush tan and do not put any lotion on.

At Appointment Time

Come to the appointment with loose, dark clothing on…or bring it with you to change into after the tan. Tight clothing can rub off the DHA bronzer & smear the cosmetic bronzer. Do not wear jeans of any kind & avoid silk & polyesters if you can help it. The bronzer will wash out of most fabrics but not so well with the ones just listed! If you come with makeup on, we have enzyme wipes that will remove it nicely. This will also remove any lotion you may have on your body…just in case you forgot! The tan will take about 20 minutes to apply & will take 8 hours to develop. Bombshell does not supply any disposable undergarments as they don’t always leave the best tan lines. We suggest that you bring in some small thongs of your own or tan in the buff.

Immediately After

You should avoid activities that may cause you to sweat excessively…use the air conditioning in your car on a hot day as you want to avoid perspiring all together. Any water that comes into contact with the skin will stop the DHA process & the skin from tanning….so if it’s raining during your appointment time, be sure to bring an umbrella! If you have leather seats in your car and you are wearing shorts, throw a towel down before sitting down…same with sofas.

After Tan Develops

The first time you get in the shower after your airbrush tan, you will just want to rinse off. You can wash your private areas with soap & a washcloth but not anywhere else. When the water runs clear, that means the bronzer guide should be off the skin just as it is designed to do. Please note that the bronzer is a guide for the technician applying the spray tan so we can see where we have sprayed you! When getting out of the shower & drying off, blot or dab your skin…never rub. If you have lighter colored towels, you may see some color transfer…this is normal & will wash out. At this time, you may apply a nice moisturizing body lotion.

Maintaining Your Airbrush Tan

Your Bombshell Airbrush Tan can last up to 7 days. Make sure that you moisturize, moisturize, moisturize!!! Taking long baths and chlorine from pools & jacuzzis can cause your tan to fade faster. If you want to keep the glow going, feel free to apply a body lotion with self tanner. Bombshell carries a fantastic line that we think you will love for face & body. We also offer special body scrubs to use in the shower…made for your airbrush tan to keep it fresh & bright!!

Bombshell Airbrush Tans are NEVER orange. Your skin develops into a beautiful caramel color no matter what your skin tone without any streaking or splotches…this ain’t no Mystic Tan baby!!!
